Programme

As TTC is a contest and not a workshop or conference, it has a rather unusual format. The morning of the contest will be used to score the solutions to the offline cases (FIXML and Movie Database). The afternoon of the contest will be used to score solutions to the live contest (a transformation problem that will be announced on Monday 21st July). The purpose of the morning and afternoon sessions is the same: to present your solution, score the solutions of others, and to crown an overall winner in each category.

Monday 21st July

1400 - 1405 : The live contest will be announced in PX001, and the supporting resources (metamodels, models, testcases, etc.) will be made available.

Tuesday 22nd July

0900 - 0905 : The live contest announcement will be repeated in PX001.

Monday 21st July, Tuesday 22nd July, Wednesday 23rd July, Thursday 24th July

All day - Hackspace will be available (location TBC) for you to work on your solutions to the live contest, or to talk about transformations with other contestants and TTC organisers.
