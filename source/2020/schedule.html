Programme

This year, TTC is being run as an online event. While the OCL2SQL case has been postponed to TTC'21, the Roundtrip case has received a number of solutions, and as such will cover the whole event. In order to participate, it is necessary to fill in the appropriate registration form. Attendance is free.

Taking into account the registrations after the close of the peer reviews, July 17th 2020 @ 3-5pm CEST was chosen to run the event.

Registrations will continue to be open until July 16th 9pm CEST. At that time, we will review any new registrations and expand the calendar invitation as appropriate.
